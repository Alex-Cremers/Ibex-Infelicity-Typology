Hello, and thank you for your participation!


Task: In this experiment, you will read a series of sentences. Your task will be to judge whether each sentence is true or not. You will answer the question “Do you think the sentence is true?” by adjusting a slider between “definitely not” and “definitely”.


Example 1

“Alaska is the northernmost US state.”

Do you think the sentence is true?

In this example, you would give a positive answer, since we know the sentence is true.

Example 2

“Dogs fly better than seagulls.”

Do you think the sentence is true?

In this example, you would give a negative answer, because the sentence is clearly false.


Before you start:

  • You will give your responses using a slider. The slider follows your cursor and clicking validates your answer.
  • At the end of the survey, after submitting your responses, you will receive a code to copy into Prolific to validate your participation.
  • The survey should take 5 to 10 min.
  • Sometimes, it will not be very clear whether the sentence is true or false, and some sentences might sound a bit odd. In any case, follow your intuition.
